What's MSYP?

The MSYP Congress will be hosted with one of the most prestigious international event held in Morocco : IEEE international Congress on Information Science and Technology (CIST 22) in December 2023 in Agadir .

IEEE MSYP Congress seeks to bring together engineering students and professionals to provide technical enrichment and ideas exchange. This event will be a unique opportunity to skill-building, networking, and collaborating on innovative ideas . We have a mission to help advancing technology for Humanity and a vision to scale up entrepreneurship skills and education .

Objectives, Highlights and Participants

The event aims to develop participants' skills through workshops and talks by distinguished speakers, facilitate networking activities to meet new people and collaborate on projects, and discover new cultures with participants from Europe, the Middle East, and Africa, creating an amazing atmosphere. Highlights of the event include technical talks and tutorials, training sessions, an award ceremony, and a social event. Participants include students from academia, faculties, and engineering schools, young professionals in ICT, technology, and engineering sectors, as well as young entrepreneurs integrating technology into businesses.
